Limonana

Description

This jubilant refreshment is quaffed throughout the Middle East in the hot months of summer.

Ingredients

  • 3.5 lemons (or 5 limes)
  • 10g mint leaves
  • 100-130g sugar
  • 700ml cold water
  • 150g ice

Instructions

  1. Take the lemons, mint leaves, sugar and cold water in hand and sling them into a blender of good whipping power.
  2. Whir the ingredients at low speed to combine, then excite on the high setting no less than thirty seconds to fully thrash the mint. In this time the mixture should become foamy and powerfully green.
  3. You may now add more sugar should you desire, and you may add the ice and blend again.
  4. Pour through a fine strainer and into a glass. Serve cold, over ice.
